Au FCC

Phonon band structure for Au (see Examples/Phonon_bands/Au_FCC).

Lattice parameter a = 4.08 Å and a 6x6x6 repetition of the primitive cell. Main computational settings:

PAO.EnergyShift 0.01 Ry
PAO.BasisSize   DZP
XC.functional   GGA
XC.authors      PBE
MeshCutoff      500. Ry
MD.FCDispl      0.02 Ang
