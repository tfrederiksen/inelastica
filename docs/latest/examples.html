Examples

The following physical systems are used to exemplify various use cases of Inelastica:

Bulk systems

Calculation of electron and phonon band structures, density-of-states, etc.

  • Au FCC
  • Na BCC
  • NaCl

Nanoscale electronic devices

Calculation of eigenchannels, elastic transmission, vibrations, electron-phonon couplings, IETS, etc.

  • H2 molecule bridging 1D Au electrodes
  • Graphene/SiC
  • CO/Cu(111)
  • Nudged elastic band
